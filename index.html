Action Plan 1: Fitkari Distribution

Purchased and distributed fitkari (alum) to households for purifying well water

How it works:

When added to water, alum causes particles and germs to clump and settle, making the water clearer and safer to drink

Linked Target: SDG 6.1 - Universal access to safe drinking water
Indicator: 6.1.1 - Proportion of population using safely managed drinking water services

Resources & Challenges

Resources Needed

  • Fitkari (alum) supply from local markets
  • Smartphone, internet access, and apps for creating social media posts

Challenges & Solutions

1. Limited Reach

Social media posts only reached immediate circle

Solution: Encourage community leaders, teachers, and peers to share posts widely

2. Small Budget

Limited funds restricted fitkari purchases

Solution: Purchased in bulk at low cost and sought small donations from classmates or local shops
